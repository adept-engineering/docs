# What's New in v2.0

Version 2.0 introduces five new modules that extend Kontratar from opportunity tracking and proposal generation into a full-lifecycle government business development platform.

## New modules

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="Awards" href="/Version-2/Awards">
    Browse, filter, and analyze federal contract awards sourced from USAspending.gov. Includes award analytics, entity profiles, AI Chat, Projects, and scheduled Research.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Forecasts" href="/Version-2/Forecasts">
    Browse planned future procurements that agencies have announced but not yet solicited. Includes calendar view, AI Chat, Projects, and scheduled Research.
  </Card>
  <Card title="eBuy Opportunities" href="/Version-2/eBuy-Opportunities">
    View GSA Schedule opportunities from the eBuy portal alongside SAM.gov opportunities. Includes AI-extracted requirements, partner ranking, and proposal outlines.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Pipeline Management" href="/Version-2/Pipeline-Management">
    Track opportunities through an 8-stage capture management lifecycle with dashboard metrics, funnel visualization, and stage-by-stage management.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Campaigns" href="/Version-2/Campaigns">
    Create email templates and send bulk outreach to contracting officers and teaming partners. Includes attachment token personalization and a 21-day government email cooldown rule.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## New AI capabilities

<CardGroup cols={1}>
  <Card title="AI Chat for Awards and Forecasts" href="/Version-2/Awards#ai-chat-for-awards">
    Query the awards and forecast databases in plain English. Ask questions and receive data-backed answers.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Projects" href="/Version-2/Awards#projects">
    Create scoped research workspaces with custom instructions and file uploads. The AI answers only within the context you define.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Scheduled Research" href="/Version-2/Awards#research">
    Set up recurring AI queries that run daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly. Results are saved by date for trend comparison and can be exported as Word, Excel, or CSV.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Changes from v1.2

All features from v1.2 remain available. Version 2.0 adds the modules listed above without removing or changing existing functionality.
