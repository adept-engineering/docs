## **What is the Pipeline?**

The **Pipeline** tracks opportunities through a structured **8-stage business development lifecycle**. It mirrors the standard federal government **capture management process**. The Pipeline ties together the full journey from when something is a **forecast**, to when it becomes an **opportunity**, to when it is **awarded**, with **charts and links** connecting every stage.

**How to get there:** Sidebar → Click **"Pipeline"** → This expands to show **"Dashboard"** and **"Funnel"**

## **The 8 Pipeline Stages**

- **Stage 1 Market Identification:** Identifying relevant **markets, agencies, and procurement trends**. Deciding which sectors to pursue.
- **Stage 2 Long-Term Positioning:** Building **agency relationships**, attending **industry days**, and positioning your company as a **known entity**.
- **Stage 3 Opportunity ID & Qualification:** Identified a specific opportunity and making your **bid or no-bid decision**.
- **Stage 4 Capture Planning:** Developing your **win strategy**, identifying **teaming partners**, and understanding how the government will evaluate proposals.
- **Stage 5 Proposal Planning:** Outlining the **proposal structure**, assigning **writers**, and setting **internal deadlines**.
- **Stage 6 Proposal Development:** Writing, reviewing, and refining the **actual proposal content**.
- **Stage 7 Submission & Negotiation:** Submitted the **proposal** and handling any government **clarification requests or negotiations**.
- **Stage 8 Contract Award & Management:** Won the **contract** and managing **execution and performance**.

## **Using the Pipeline Dashboard**

**How to get there:** Sidebar → Pipeline → Click **"Dashboard"**

- Click **Dashboard** under Pipeline in the sidebar.
- At the top, you see four **metric cards**: **Total Records** (total opportunities in your pipeline), **Active** (currently being pursued), **Won** (resulted in a contract award), and **Win Rate** (percentage won).
- Below the metrics, a **funnel chart** shows how many opportunities sit at each of the 8 stages along with their **combined dollar value per stage**.
- Use this view to **spot bottlenecks**, for example if many opportunities are stuck at **Capture Planning** but few are progressing to **Proposal Development**.

## **Managing Pipeline Records**

**How to get there:** Sidebar → Pipeline → Click **"Funnel"**

- Click **Funnel** under Pipeline in the sidebar. This shows a **table of all your pipeline records**.
- The table has columns: **Title, Agency, Stage, and Actions**.
- To add a **new record**, click the **Create** button, or add an opportunity from any opportunity detail page in the **Opportunities section**.
- To **move a record** to a different stage, click the **Actions menu** on that record's row and select the target stage.
- To **edit a record's details**, click **Actions** and select **Edit**.
- To **remove a record**, click **Actions** and select **Delete**.
