## **What are Campaigns?**

**Campaigns** let you send **bulk outreach emails** to contracting officers, potential teaming partners, or other contacts. The system has two components: **Templates** (which define the email content) and **Campaigns** (which send a template to recipients). Campaigns can be sent to **government officials** as well as **private individuals**.

**How to get there:** Sidebar → Workspace → Click **"Campaigns"**

## **Campaign Templates**

A **template** defines the structure and content of your outreach email. Once created, a template can be **reused across multiple campaigns**. You do not have to recreate the email content each time.

**Creating a Template**

- On the Campaigns page, make sure the **Templates** tab is selected.
- Click **Create New Template**.
- Enter a **Template Name**. Use something descriptive, for example, "541511_SUBSCRIPTION" or "AES_8A_REACHOUT".
- Set the **Status** to **Active**. Only Active templates can be selected when sending a campaign. Set it to **Inactive** if you want to save a draft that is not ready to use yet.
- Write the **Email Subject** — this is the subject line recipients see in their inbox.
- Write the **email Body**. This is the main content. Write it generically so it works for all recipients in a campaign.
- Optionally, **upload an Attachment**. Drag and drop a file into the upload zone or click to browse. Supported formats: PDF, TXT, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX.
- Click **Create Template** to save.

**Customizable Tokens in Attachments**

- Campaign attachments support **four customizable token fields**.
- When you embed these tokens inside a PDF, CSV, or other attachment file, Kontratar automatically replaces each token with the **recipient-specific** or **campaign-specific data** when the campaign is sent.
- This lets you **personalize attached documents** without creating a separate file for each person.

**Managing Existing Templates**

- On the **Templates tab**, you see a table listing all your templates with columns: **Name, Status** (Active or Inactive), and **Attachment** (shows the filename if attached, or a dash if none).
- To **edit a template**, click **View/Edit** on that row. You can update the **name, subject, body, or attachment**.
- To **delete a template permanently**, click **Delete** on that row.
- To **temporarily disable a template** without deleting it, edit it and switch the **Status** to **Inactive**.

## **Sending a Campaign**

- On the Campaigns page, click the **Campaigns** tab.
- Click **Create New Campaign**.
- Select a **Template** from the dropdown. Only templates with **Active** status appear here.
- Enter the **recipient email addresses**. You can type a single address or paste multiple addresses for bulk outreach.
- Review the **preview** showing the subject line, body, and attachment.
- Click **Send**. The email is dispatched simultaneously to every email address you entered.

## **The 21-Day Government Email Rule**

- When you send a campaign to a **government email address**, Kontratar tracks the send date.
- If you try to send another campaign to that same **government email address** within 21 days, the system displays a **warning message** before allowing you to proceed.
- This exists because **government agencies actively monitor for unsolicited bulk emails**. Sending multiple emails to the same contracting officer in a short window may get your email address **flagged as spam or blocked**.
- The **21-day cooling period** protects your **sender reputation**. You can override the warning and send anyway, but you do so **at your own risk**.

## **Campaign Use Cases**

**Example 1 Expiring Software Contract Outreach**

- Go to Sidebar → Forecasts. Search for **software contracts expiring** within the next year.
- Collect the **contracting officer email addresses** from the forecast detail pages.
- Go to Sidebar → Campaigns. Create a template highlighting your **software renewal capabilities**. Attach your **Capability Statement PDF**.
- Create a **new Campaign** using that template. Paste in the contracting officer emails.
- Click **Send**.

**Example 2 Targeted Industry Capability Outreach**

- Go to Sidebar → Forecasts. Search for agencies or companies currently using **IBM software**.
- Gather the relevant **contact email addresses**.
- Go to Sidebar → Campaigns. Create a template emphasizing your **IBM expertise**. Attach your **Capability Statement**.
- Create a **Campaign**, paste in the email addresses, and **send**.
