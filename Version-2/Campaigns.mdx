**Campaigns **let you send bulk outreach emails to contracting officers, potential teaming partners, or other contacts. The system has two components: **Templates** (which define the email content) and **Campaigns** (which send a template to a list of recipients). Campaigns can be sent to government officials and private individuals.

**How to get there:** Sidebar → Workspace → Click **Campaigns**

## Campaign templates

A template defines the structure and content of your outreach email. Once created, a template can be reused across multiple campaigns.

### Creating a template

1. On the Campaigns page, select the **Templates** tab.
2. Click **Create New Template**.
3. Fill in the following fields:

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Template Name** | A descriptive name (for example, "541511\_SUBSCRIPTION" or "AES\_8A\_REACHOUT"). |
| **Status** | Set to **Active** to make the template available for campaigns. Set to **Inactive** to save as a draft. |
| **Email Subject** | The subject line recipients see in their inbox. |
| **Body** | The email content. Write it generically so it works for all recipients in a campaign. |
| **Attachment** | Optional. Drag and drop a file or click to browse. Supported formats: PDF, TXT, DOC, DOCX, XLS, XLSX. |

4. Click **Create Template** to save.

### Customizable tokens in attachments

Campaign attachments support four customizable token fields. When you embed these tokens inside a PDF, CSV, or other attachment file, Kontratar replaces each token with the recipient-specific or campaign-specific data when the campaign is sent. This lets you personalize attached documents without creating a separate file for each recipient.

### Managing templates

The Templates tab shows a table of all your templates with columns: Name, Status (Active or Inactive), and Attachment (filename or dash if none).

| Action | How to do it |
| --- | --- |
| **Edit a template** | Click **View/Edit** on the template row. Update the name, subject, body, or attachment. |
| **Delete a template** | Click **Delete** on the template row. This action is permanent. |
| **Disable a template** | Edit the template and switch Status to **Inactive**. The template is preserved but cannot be selected for new campaigns. |

## Sending a campaign

1. On the Campaigns page, click the **Campaigns** tab.
2. Click **Create New Campaign**.
3. Select a **Template** from the dropdown. Only templates with Active status appear.
4. Enter recipient email addresses. You can type a single address or paste multiple addresses for bulk outreach.
5. Review the preview showing the subject line, body, and attachment.
6. Click **Send**. The email is dispatched to every address you entered.

## The 21-day government email rule

When you send a campaign to a government email address, Kontratar tracks the send date. If you attempt to send another campaign to the same government email address within 21 days, the system displays a warning before allowing you to proceed.

This restriction exists because government agencies monitor for unsolicited bulk emails. Sending multiple emails to the same contracting officer in a short window may cause your email address to be flagged as spam or blocked.

The 21-day cooling period protects your sender reputation. You can override the warning and send anyway, but you do so at your own risk.

## Campaign use cases

### Expiring software contract outreach

1. Go to **Forecasts**. Search for software contracts expiring within the next year.
2. Collect contracting officer email addresses from the forecast detail pages.
3. Go to **Campaigns**. Create a template highlighting your software renewal capabilities. Attach your Capability Statement PDF.
4. Create a new Campaign using that template. Paste in the contracting officer emails.
5. Click **Send**.

### Targeted industry capability outreach

1. Go to **Forecasts**. Search for agencies or companies currently using a specific technology (for example, IBM software).
2. Gather relevant contact email addresses.
3. Go to **Campaigns**. Create a template emphasizing your expertise with that technology. Attach your Capability Statement.
4. Create a Campaign, paste in the email addresses, and send.
