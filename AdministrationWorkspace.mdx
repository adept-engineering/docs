## **Administration Sections Overview**

Under the Administration tab, you will find the following key sections:

**Users**

- **Super Admins** can:
  - Deactivate user accounts
  - Reset user passwords
  - View a full list of organization users
- **Tenant Admins** can:
  - Invite new users to their organization
  - Manage team roles and permissions

⚠️ Note: For security, only one **Tenant Admin** is allowed per organization.

**Proposals**

- View and manage **all proposals** generated by your organization.
- Track drafts, completed submissions, and statuses in one place.

**Partners**

- Create and manage **partner companies** you collaborate.
- Upload each partner’s **capability statement** (PDF or doc) that outlines their service strengths and opportunity focus.
- Add and manage:
  - Documents tied to each partner
  - Partner websites (Kontratar will auto-scrape public info to support analysis)
  - Opportunity categories linked to that partner

> Use this section to match specific partners with contract types based on their past performance and strengths.

**Simulation**

- Run **opportunity simulations** to assess your organization’s fit.
- The system evaluates your **strengths and weaknesses** for any selected opportunity.
- Simulations are based on category alignment, capability documents, and past activity.

> Click **“View Categories”** under each partner to see what they’re best suited for.

**Agencies**

- Add, edit, or remove **government agencies** relevant to your contracts.
- Maintain an up-to-date list of agencies your organization actively tracks or pursues.
- Use agency names and details for filtering and proposal generation workflows.

<Note>
  Changes are implemented after 48hrs
</Note>

**Team**

- Manage internal teams assigned to different contracts or tasks.
- Create teams based on departments, roles, or opportunity focus.
- Assign team responsibilities to streamline proposal workflows or partner coordination.

## **Categories**

The **Categories** section allows you to manage classification tags tied to documents and partner profiles.

Here’s what you can do:

- **View all existing categories** in your organization
- **Search for categories** using keywords
- **Add documents** to specific categories for better organization and filtering
- **Remove documents** from categories as needed

> Categories help in structuring simulations, matching capabilities with contract types, and streamlining partner selection.

### Documents

The **Documents** section allows your organization to manage all stored files in one centralized location.

Here’s what you can do:

- **View all previously uploaded documents**
- **Upload new documents** relevant to proposals, partners, or simulations
- **Tag documents** with appropriate **categories** for better organization and retrieval
- **Reuse documents** across multiple opportunities, saving time and effort

> Keeping documents organized here ensures fast access during proposal generation and partner collaboration.
