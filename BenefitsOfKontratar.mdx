title: "Benefits of Kontratar"
---

This section describes the operational outcomes reported by organizations using Kontratar for government contracting workflows.

## Proposal turnaround time

Kontratar reduces the time required to produce a government proposal. The AI generates structured, compliant drafts from solicitation data and organizational documents, replacing the manual process of writing proposals from scratch. Organizations using the platform have reported an 85% reduction in proposal turnaround time.

## Opportunity matching

The platform filters and ranks opportunities based on your organization's NAICS codes, capabilities, and past performance data. This replaces the manual process of searching SAM.gov and evaluating each listing individually. The ranking system directs attention to opportunities with the highest relevance to your organization.

## Government monitoring

Kontratar monitors federal procurement sources and delivers updates when new solicitations, amendments, or awards are published. This replaces the manual process of checking SAM.gov and other databases for changes.

## Team collaboration

Multiple team members can work on a proposal at the same time. The platform provides role-based access, version control, section-level editor assignment, and project management controls (due dates, deadlines, reminders). This reduces coordination overhead when multiple contributors are involved in a proposal.

## Compliance

Proposals are generated with compliance requirements extracted from the solicitation. Passphrase encryption protects proposal documents. Multi-factor authentication protects user accounts. Role-based access and feature locks control who can view and modify sensitive data.

## Decision support

Built-in analytics track opportunity volume, award trends, and proposal status across the organization. Team Simulation provides a data-based assessment of organizational fit before committing to a bid. These tools replace informal bid/no-bid decisions with structured evaluation.

## Cost

By automating opportunity research, requirement extraction, and proposal drafting, Kontratar reduces the labor hours required per proposal. Organizations have reported a 40% reduction in proposal development costs, allowing more proposals to be submitted with the same team.
