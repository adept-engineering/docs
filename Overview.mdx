title: "Overview"
description: "Kontratar is a federal procurement intelligence and proposal generation platform. It aggregates government contract opportunities, evaluates organizational fit, and generates compliant proposals."
---

The platform supports the following functions:

- Aggregates contract opportunities from SAM.gov and user-defined custom sources
- Filters opportunities by NAICS code, agency, date, and set-aside type
- Tracks opportunities through a personal workspace (My Desk) with pipeline management
- Generates three types of proposal output: full proposals, table of contents outlines, and extracted requirements
- Tests organizational fit against specific opportunities through team simulation
- Manages proposal editing, collaboration, and export across teams
- Stores and categorizes company documents for reuse across proposals
- Provides administrative controls for users, partners, teams, and agencies

Kontratar is used by federal contractors, state and local government vendors, small businesses pursuing set-aside contracts, and business development teams managing multi-agency pipelines.
