---
title: "Team Simulation"
---

Team Simulation evaluates how well your organization or a teaming partner aligns with a specific government opportunity. The system analyzes your uploaded documents, company profile, and partner data against the opportunity's solicitation requirements to produce a capability assessment.

Use Team Simulation to:

- Make bid or no-bid decisions based on data rather than estimates
- Compare multiple teaming partners to identify the strongest fit for a specific opportunity
- Identify capability gaps before starting proposal development

<Info>
  **Before you begin:** For accurate simulation results, upload relevant company documents (past performance narratives, capability statements, resumes) in the [Documents](/Documents) section. The simulation uses these files as input for the capability assessment.
</Info>

## How to run a simulation

1. Go to the **Simulation** section in the Workspace panel.

![Simulation section in the Workspace panel](images/Screenshot\(96\).png)

2. Click **Generate Simulation**.

![Generate Simulation button](images/Screenshot\(97\).png)

3. Enter a **simulation title**.

![Simulation title entry field](images/Screenshot\(99\).png)

4. Choose the source of the opportunity:

| Source | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **SAM** | Federal opportunities sourced from SAM.gov. |
| **Custom** | Opportunities created manually by your organization. |

![Opportunity source selection](images/Screenshot\(100\).png)

5. Select the **specific opportunity** you want to simulate against.
6. Select the **client** — your own company or a partner organization.

![Client selection screen](images/Screenshot\(98\).png)

7. Click **Generate**.

## Simulation results

After generation, the results screen displays:

- The **requirements** extracted from the selected opportunity
- A **capability assessment** based on your uploaded documents, company profile, and past activity
- An **alignment evaluation** comparing your capabilities to the opportunity's requirements

![Simulation results showing capability assessment](images/image\(4\).jpg)

You can run multiple simulations against different partners for the same opportunity. Compare the results to determine which team or partner has the strongest alignment.

## What to do next

After reviewing simulation results:

- If the results indicate strong alignment, proceed to [Proposal Generation](/ProposalGeneration) to create a response.
- If the results show capability gaps, consider adding a teaming partner through the [Administrator Workspace](/AdministrationWorkspace) Partners section, or upload additional documents to improve your profile.
