### AI Chatbot Assistant

* View the opportunity and engage in conversation.

* The chatbot functions like any standard LLM-based app, such as ChatGPT. No specific commands are required, just type naturally.

* Ask the chatbot for assistance with proposal writing, formatting, or best practices.

* Ensure your questions are clear and explicitly state what you expect from the chatbot for the best results.
