<Info>
  **Before you begin**

  You need the following to create an account:

  - A work email address. Personal email addresses (Gmail, Yahoo, Outlook.com) are not accepted.
  - Your company's legal name, domain, and mailing address.

  Only one person per company can register through the signup page. That person becomes the primary admin. All other users must be invited by the primary admin.
</Info>

## Creating an account

To create a new Kontratar account:

1. Go to [app.kontratar.com](https://app.kontratar.com).
2. Click **Log In**, then select **Sign Up**.

![Kontratar login page](images/Screenshot\(146\).png)

3. Enter your company details.

![Kontratar registration form](images/Screenshot\(147\).png)

The registration form requires the following company information:

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| Company name | Your organization's legal or operating name. |
| Company domain | Your company's website domain (for example, `skyrimtech.com`). This domain must not already be registered in Kontratar. |
| Address | Your company's street address. |
| Country | Country of registration. |
| State | State or province. |
| City | City. |
| ZIP code | Postal or ZIP code. |

4. Enter your personal details.

| Field | Description |
| --- | --- |
| First name | Your given name. |
| Last name | Your surname. |
| Email address | A work email that matches your company domain. |
| Password | A password for your Kontratar account. |

5. Click **Submit**.

If the registration is successful, your account is created and you are logged in. You become the primary admin for your organization.

<Warning>
  **Registration will fail if:**

  - The email address is not a work email.
  - The company domain has already been registered by another user.
  - A personal email provider is used (Gmail, Yahoo, etc.).

  If your company domain is already registered, contact your organization's primary admin to request an invitation. See [Administrator Workspace](/AdministrationWorkspace) for instructions on inviting users.
</Warning>

## Navigating the dashboard

After logging in, the Home Dashboard is displayed. The dashboard shows:

- **Charts and visualizations** — A summary of government contract activity filtered by your selected NAICS codes.
- **Opportunity count** — The number of active opportunities matching your filters, organized by agency.
- **Awards and forecasts** — Data on recent contract awards and forecasted procurement activity.

The left sidebar provides access to all platform features:

| Sidebar item | Function |
| --- | --- |
| **Home** | Displays the main dashboard with summary charts and metrics. |
| **Opportunities** | Lists government contract opportunities from SAM.gov and custom sources. Contains two tabs: **SAM** and **Custom**. |
| **My Desk** | Shows opportunities you have tracked, with options to generate proposals or discard items. Contains three tabs: Available Opportunities, Tracked Opportunities, and Discarded Opportunities. |
| **Team Simulation** | Tests how well your organization or a teaming partner aligns with a specific opportunity's requirements. |
| **Documents** | Stores company files used during proposal generation (past performance narratives, capability statements, resumes). |
| **Categories** | Manages classification tags applied to documents and partner profiles. |
| **Administrator** | Organizational management panel for users, partners, teams, agencies, and settings. Visible only to Super Admins and Tenant Admins. |
| **Settings** | Configures NAICS codes, notification preferences, and display options. |
| **Proposal Generation** | Creates AI-generated proposals from tracked opportunities. |

## What to do next

After your account is set up:

- **Configure your NAICS codes** — Go to **Settings** or the **Administrator \> NAICS Code** section to add the NAICS codes that match your business. These codes control which opportunities appear in your dashboard and Opportunities tab.
- **Upload company documents** — Go to **Documents** and upload files that describe your organization's capabilities. These files are used by the AI during proposal generation. Recommended document types include past performance narratives, capability statements, and key personnel resumes.
- **Track an opportunity** — Go to **Opportunities**, browse or filter the listings, and click **Track** on any opportunity you want to pursue. Tracked opportunities appear in **My Desk**.
- **Invite your team** — If you are the primary admin, go to **Administrator \> Users** to invite other members of your organization.
