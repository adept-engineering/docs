- **What is Kontratar?** Kontratar is an AI-powered platform purpose-built for government business development. By unifying capture management, proposal generation, and resource planning into a single, seamless workspace, Kontratar delivers end-to-end automation across the government contracting lifecycle, eliminating the need for disconnected, legacy systems.
- **How is Kontratar’s AI different from other proposal tools?** Kontratar is architected with AI at its core. Unlike platforms that simply layer AI onto existing systems, every function within Kontratar is powered by advanced machine learning. Our generative AI produces compliant, agency-ready proposals that reflect a deep understanding of government requirements and continuously refines outputs based on your organization’s performance.
- **Who benefits from using Kontratar?** Kontratar is designed for government vendors of all types and sizes, including federal contractors, state and local suppliers, small businesses, and large enterprises managing complex, multi-agency portfolios. Whether you are pursuing your first opportunity or scaling established operations, Kontratar adapts to your needs.
- **How does AI-driven proposal generation work?  **Our platform analyzes solicitation documents, extracts compliance criteria, and leverages your company’s capabilities and performance data to automatically generate structured, professional proposals. Kontratar produces technical volumes, management sections, and supporting documentation, ensuring alignment with agency standards and requirements.
- **Can teams collaborate in real-time on proposals? **Absolutely. Kontratar provides a secure, collaborative environment where multiple team members can work simultaneously on different proposal sections. Role-based access, version control, and AI-driven consistency checks ensure efficient teamwork and content integrity throughout the process.
- **How is data security and compliance managed? **Security is foundational to Kontratar’s architecture. All data is encrypted and protected by Zero Trust security protocols, with strict role-based access controls. The platform is fully compliant with NIST AI RMF, HIPAA, and FISMA requirements. Your data remains isolated to your organization and is never used to train external models.
- **Which government databases does Kontratar monitor? **Kontratar integrates directly with SAM, eBuy, and other critical government procurement systems, providing real-time monitoring of new opportunities, amendments, and award notifications, ensuring you always have access to the most current information.
- **How accurate is Kontratar’s opportunity matching?** Our AI analyzes your organization’s capabilities, win history, and market positioning to provide ranked opportunity recommendations based on the probability of success. The system factors in agency preferences, competitive landscape, and relevant market intelligence to deliver actionable insights.
- **Does Kontratar support Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR)?** Yes, Kontratar includes dedicated features for SBIR opportunities. The platform matches your research focus to SBIR topics, generates proposal outlines, and assists with commercialization planning, helping innovative businesses secure research funding.
- **Can Kontratar integrate with our existing business systems?** Integration is seamless. Kontratar offers robust API connectivity to document management systems, CRM platforms, and business intelligence tools, allowing you to maintain existing workflows while gaining the benefits of advanced AI automation.
- **How quickly can we get started with Kontratar? **Most organizations are up and running within 24 hours of account setup. Our intuitive onboarding process guides you through profile creation, platform configuration, and opportunity identification, so you can start generating proposals immediately.
- **What support is available for Kontratar users?** We provide comprehensive support, including AI-powered chat assistance, personalized demonstration sessions, and direct access to our technical team. Emergency support is available 24/7 to help you meet critical deadlines.
- **How is Kontratar priced? **Our pricing model is flexible and scales with your organization’s needs, accommodating everyone from single users to large enterprise teams. Plans are based on user count, proposal generation volume, and required feature sets. Contact us for a custom pricing assessment.
- **How does the AI improve over time? **- Kontratar’s AI continually learns from your organization’s results, refining recommendations and outputs based on proposal performance, win rates, and market feedback. All learning is organization-specific to maintain strict data privacy and security.
- **What happens to our data if we discontinue service? ** Your data remains your property at all times. Should you choose to discontinue service, Kontratar provides secure export options for all proposals, opportunity data, and analytics, ensuring your business continuity.
- **How does Kontratar handle varying agency requirements? **The platform maintains detailed agency profiles, tracking evaluation criteria, preferences, and award patterns. This intelligence enables the AI to tailor proposals and recommendations for maximum alignment with agency expectations.
- **Can we customize AI outputs to fit our brand and voice? **Yes. Kontratar adapts to your organization’s terminology, style, and strategic positioning, ensuring all outputs reflect your unique voice while maintaining compliance with government requirements.
- **Which browsers support push notifications for Kontratar? **Kontratar’s push notifications are supported on the following browsers for desktop and Android devices:

  Google Chrome

  Mozilla Firefox

  Microsoft Edge

  Opera

  Samsung Internet
- **Are push notifications available on iOS devices?** Not at the moment. Due to Apple’s restrictions, all iOS browsers are built on Safari’s WebKit engine, which does not currently support push notifications. We’ll update users once Apple expands support in future releases.
