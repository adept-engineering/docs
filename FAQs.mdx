### **1. What is Kontratar?**

Kontratar is an AI-powered platform designed to streamline the government contracting process. It provides real-time government contract updates, AI-driven proposal generation, contract matching, team collaboration, and compliance automation, helping businesses improve efficiency and increase their chances of winning contracts.

### **2. Who is Kontratar for?**

Kontratar is built for businesses of all sizes looking to secure government contracts. Kontratar helps simplify and optimize your business development process.

### **3. How does Kontratar help with proposal generation?**

Kontratar automates proposal writing by leveraging AI to analyze contract requirements and generate tailored, compliant proposals. This reduces manual effort, improves accuracy, and speeds up the process by up to 85%.

### **4. How does Kontratar match my business with the right contracts?**

Kontratar’s AI-driven Contract Matching & Ranking feature recommends the most relevant opportunities based on your company’s past performance, industry trends, and business profile. This helps you focus on contracts you’re most likely to win.

### **5. Can multiple team members work on a proposal at the same time?**

Yes\! Our Team Collaboration and Simulation feature allows multiple users to work on a proposal in real time, with role-based access, version control, and streamlined workflows for better efficiency.

### **6. How does Kontratar ensure proposal security and compliance?**

Kontratar follows a Zero Trust Compliance approach. Proposal generation can only be accessed by the creator of the proposal. Even we cannot access any proposal. All proposals are encrypted using advanced encryption and role-based access controls to protect sensitive business data.

### **7. How do I get started with Kontratar?**

Simply sign up, enter your company details, and start exploring contract opportunities. Our AI-powered chatbot is available to assist you through every step of the process.

### **8. Is there a free trial available?**

Yes\! Kontratar offers a free trial so you can experience its features before committing.

### **9. Does Kontratar only offer a subscription model?**

No, Kontratar offers more than just a subscription model. In addition to our monthly plans, we provide:

- Private Install - A one-time purchase option where you can install and host Kontratar on your own infrastructure.
- Dedicated Install - A fully hosted, private environment managed by us, ensuring data security and isolation. [Contact our sales team for more details.](https://cal.com/kontratarsupport/sales-call)

### **10.  Which browsers support push notifications for Kontratar?**

Kontratar’s push notifications are supported on the following browsers for desktop and Android devices:

- Google Chrome
- Mozilla Firefox
- Microsoft Edge
- Opera
- Samsung Internet

### **10. Are push notifications available on iOS devices?**

Not at the moment. Due to Apple’s restrictions, all iOS browsers are built on Safari’s WebKit engine, which does not currently support push notifications.

We’ll update users once Apple expands support in future releases.
