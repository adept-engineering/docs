## Selecting a plan

1. Go to [kontratar.com](https://kontratar.com).
2. Click **Pricing** in the top navigation menu.
3. Review the available subscription plans.
4. Select the plan that matches your organization's needs.

## Completing payment

1. After selecting a plan, you are redirected to the Stripe payment gateway.

![Stripe payment gateway](images/image\(9\).webp)

2. Enter your payment details (credit card, debit card, or other payment method supported by Stripe).
3. Review the payment details for accuracy.
4. Click **Subscribe for your preferred amount** to complete the transaction.

## After payment

Once payment is processed:

- You receive a confirmation email from Stripe.
- Your account is upgraded to the selected plan.
- Paid features become available immediately.

![Subscription confirmation](images/image\(10\).webp)

## Managing your subscription

To view or change your subscription:

1. Log in to Kontratar.
2. Go to **Administrator \> Manage Plans** to view your current plan, resource usage, and available features.
3. Go to **Administrator \> Billing History** to view past invoices and payment records.

For subscription changes (upgrades, downgrades, or cancellations), contact support at [info@kontratar.com](mailto:info@kontratar.com) or (888) 392-3378.
