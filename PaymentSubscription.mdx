### **1. Access the Kontratar Website Page**

- Click on the provided link to navigate to the [Kontratar landing page](https://kontratar.com/).

![Screenshot(26) Pn](/images/Screenshot\(26\).png)

### 2. Locate the Pricing Section

- Click on \*\*Pricing \*\*from the menu on the website.
- Select your subscription plan.

### **3. Complete Your Payment via Stripe**

- Upon selecting your preferred plan, you will be redirected to our secure Stripe payment gateway.

![Image(9) Web](/images/image\(9\).webp)

- Enter your payment details, including credit/debit card information or any other preferred. payment method supported by Stripe.

### **5. Confirm and Process the Payment**

- Review your payment details to ensure accuracy.
- Click the ‘Subscribe for \$ amount’ button to complete your transaction..

### **6. Enjoy Kontratar Premium Benefits**

- Once the payment is successfully processed, you will receive a confirmation email.
- You now have full access to Kontratar's premium features.
